sequences containing alternatively letter followed
by digit, and calculate the sum of all those
digits.

Example:

For file a.txt containing the lines below, the
result should be 1+2+3+4+5+6+6+6+7+7+7+9+9+9 = 81

m6m6m6
u7u7u7 efo 8r9r9r9t
